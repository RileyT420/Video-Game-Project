Minecraft

Minecraft is a video game in which players create and break apart various kinds of blocks in three-dimensional worlds. The game's two main modes are Survival and Creative. In Survival, players must create their own building, fetch their own supplies and food. They also interact with blocklike mobs, or moving creatures. In creative players have an infinite amount of blocks and supplies and can create whatever they want.

Super Mario Bros

One of the most iconic and nostalgic games of all time is Super Mario Bros. Super Mario Bros is a platform game. In the game, Mario must race through the Mushroom Kingdom and save Princess Toadstool (later Princess Peach) from Bowser. Mario jumps, runs, and walks across each level. The worlds are full of enemies and platforms, and open holes.
